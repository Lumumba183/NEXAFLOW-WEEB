What Is WhatsApp Automation?

WhatsApp automation uses the WhatsApp Business API to send automated, personalized messages to your customers. Unlike the free WhatsApp Business app (which is limited), the API allows you to:

  • Send bulk messages to thousands of customers at once
  • Automate responses to common questions 24/7
  • Integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce, and CRMs
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and reminders
  • Recover abandoned carts with personalized follow-ups
  • Handle multi-agent conversations with a shared team inbox

At NexaFlow Digital, we build complete WhatsApp automation workflows tailored to your business. From simple welcome messages to complex e-commerce integrations.

WhatsApp Automation for E-commerce

For online stores, WhatsApp automation is a game-changer. Here's a typical customer journey:

  1. Customer clicks "WhatsApp Us" on your Shopify store
  2. Bot sends welcome message + product catalog
  3. Customer selects items and quantities
  4. Bot calculates total (including delivery fee)
  5. Customer receives M-Pesa payment prompt
  6. Bot confirms order and sends receipt
  7. Bot sends shipping updates at each stage
  8. Bot follows up 3 days later for a review

One Nairobi fashion brand we automated now processes 60% of orders entirely through WhatsApp — from inquiry to payment to delivery tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is WhatsApp Business API free?

No, it's a paid service. You pay per "conversation" (a 24-hour chat window) at approximately KSH 3–8 per conversation. However, the ROI is high — one recovered abandoned cart can cover your entire monthly cost.

Can I use my existing phone number?

Yes, but it must not be registered with regular WhatsApp. You'll need a dedicated business number. We handle the entire setup.

How long does setup take?

Meta approval takes 1–3 business days. Once approved, we can build basic flows in 3–5 days. Complex integrations take 1–2 weeks.

Can WhatsApp automation handle M-Pesa?

Yes. We integrate M-Pesa seamlessly. The bot sends payment prompts and confirms completion automatically.

What platforms can you integrate with?

Shopify, WooCommerce, WordPress, custom websites, and most CRMs. We also integrate with Google Sheets, Zapier, and Make for custom workflows.
