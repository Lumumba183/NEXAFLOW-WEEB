Our Website Development Process

Step 1: Discovery (Day 1)

We discuss your business, goals, target audience, and competitors. We identify the keywords your customers are searching for and plan the site structure.

Step 2: Design (Days 2–5)

We create a custom design mockup based on your brand. You review and approve before we build anything.

Step 3: Development (Days 6–10)

We build the site with clean, SEO-friendly code. Every page is optimized for mobile, speed, and search engines.

Step 4: Content (Days 11–12)

We add your text, images, and branding. We optimize all images and write SEO-friendly copy if needed.

Step 5: Testing & Launch (Day 13–14)

We test on multiple devices, check all links, verify speed, and launch. We also submit your sitemap to Google.

Step 6: Handover & Training

We show you how to manage your site. You get full documentation and 30 days of free support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a website cost in Kenya?

Our website packages start at KSH 6,500 for a 5-page basic site. Standard packages are KSH 12,000 and Premium packages are KSH 18,000. All include a free domain and professional email. Read our full pricing guide.

How long does it take to build a website?

A basic 5-page website takes 1–2 weeks. Standard websites take 2–3 weeks. Premium websites with CMS take 3–4 weeks. The timeline depends on how quickly you provide content and feedback.

Yes. Our basic maintenance is KSH 2,000/year and includes updates, security patches, and backups. Enhanced maintenance with priority support is KSH 2,500–5,000/month.
