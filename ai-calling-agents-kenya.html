What Are AI Calling Agents?

AI calling agents are intelligent voice systems that handle phone calls automatically. They can:

  • Answer incoming calls — greet customers, answer FAQs, and route complex issues to humans
  • Make outbound calls — follow up on leads, confirm appointments, send reminders
  • Qualify leads — ask questions, score prospects, and book meetings
  • Take orders — process orders over the phone with M-Pesa integration
  • Send SMS follow-ups — automatically text customers after calls
  • Speak Swahili and English — communicate in both languages seamlessly

Unlike traditional IVR systems ("Press 1 for sales, Press 2 for support"), AI agents understand natural language. Customers can say anything — "I want to book a haircut tomorrow at 3 PM" — and the AI handles it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI calling agents speak Swahili?

Yes. Our AI calling agents are fluent in both English and Swahili. They can detect the customer's language from the first few words and respond accordingly. We can also train the AI to speak Sheng or other local languages.

How much do AI calling agents cost?

Pricing depends on call volume and features. A basic setup starts at approximately KSH 10,000–15,000/month for up to 1,000 calls. Higher volume plans are available. We offer a free demo so you can test before committing.

Can AI handle complex customer issues?

AI handles 80–90% of routine calls automatically. For complex issues, the AI transfers to a human with full context (call transcript, customer history, issue summary). Your team only handles what requires human judgment.

Do customers know they're talking to AI?

We recommend transparency — the AI introduces itself as "NexaFlow's virtual assistant." Most customers don't mind as long as their problem is solved quickly. In fact, many prefer AI because they don't have to wait on hold.

Can AI integrate with M-Pesa?

Yes. The AI can guide customers through M-Pesa payments during the call — confirming amounts, sending payment prompts, and verifying completion. This is especially useful for e-commerce and service businesses.
