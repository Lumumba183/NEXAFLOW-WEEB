## 2. 📝 On-Page SEO Optimization

This is the work we do ON your website to make it Google-friendly and user-friendly. Every page is optimized to rank for specific keywords.

### What We Do:
- **Title Tag Optimization:** Write compelling, keyword-rich page titles that Google loves and users click
- **Meta Description Writing:** Create persuasive summaries that appear in search results and drive clicks
- **Heading Structure (H1, H2, H3):** Organize content with proper hierarchy so Google understands what each section is about
- **Content Optimization:** Ensure keywords appear naturally in the first 100 words, headings, and throughout the content
- **Image Optimization:** Compress images for fast loading, add descriptive alt text for accessibility and SEO
- **Internal Linking:** Connect related pages on your site so Google understands your site structure and passes authority
- **URL Structure:** Clean, keyword-rich URLs (e.g., `/website-design-nairobi` instead of `/page?id=123`)
- **Schema Markup (Structured Data):** Add code that tells Google exactly what each page is — business, service, FAQ, product, etc. This enables rich snippets in search results
- **Content Freshness:** Update existing content with new information, stats, and keywords
- **FAQ Sections:** Add question-and-answer sections that target "People Also Ask" queries in Google

### Deliverables:
- Optimized title tags and meta descriptions for every page
- Heading structure improvements
- Internal linking strategy implementation
- Schema markup (JSON-LD) on key pages
- Image alt text and compression
- Content optimization reports

## 3. ⚙️ Technical SEO

This is the behind-the-scenes work that makes Google crawl, understand, and index your website properly. Technical SEO is invisible to users but critical to Google.

### What We Do:
- **Site Speed Optimization:** Make your site load in under 3 seconds (critical for mobile users in Kenya)
- **Mobile Optimization:** Ensure your site works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and all screen sizes
- **Core Web Vitals:** Google's page experience metrics — LCP (largest contentful paint), FID (first input delay), CLS (cumulative layout shift)
- **Crawl Error Fixes:** Fix broken links, 404 errors, redirect chains, and server errors
- **XML Sitemap Creation & Submission:** Create a roadmap of your site for Google and submit it to Search Console
- **Robots.txt Optimization:** Guide Google on what to crawl and what to ignore
- **Canonical Tags:** Prevent duplicate content issues (e.g., when the same page has multiple URLs)
- **SSL/HTTPS Implementation:** Ensure your site is secure (Google penalizes non-HTTPS sites)
- **Structured Data Validation:** Test and fix schema markup so Google reads it correctly
- **Indexability Audit:** Ensure every important page can be indexed by Google; fix "noindex" tags accidentally blocking pages
- **Hreflang Tags:** For multilingual sites, tell Google which language each page is in
- **Site Architecture:** Ensure your site structure is logical and flat (users find pages in 3 clicks or less)
- **JavaScript SEO:** Ensure Google can read content rendered by JavaScript (React, Vue, Angular sites)

### Deliverables:
- Technical SEO audit report
- Site speed score improvements (PageSpeed Insights)
- Mobile usability fixes
- XML sitemap and robots.txt files
- Core Web Vitals optimization report
- Monthly technical health checks

## Common Questions Clients Ask

**Q: How long does SEO take to show results?**
A: For new websites, expect 3–6 months for meaningful rankings. For established sites, improvements can happen in 1–2 months. SEO is cumulative — results grow stronger over time. The first 3 months are about building foundations; months 4–6 are when you see real traffic growth.

**Q: Can you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?**
A: No honest SEO agency can guarantee #1 rankings because Google controls the algorithm. What we guarantee is: best-practice execution, transparent reporting, and steady improvement. Most of our clients see page 1 rankings for multiple keywords within 3–6 months. We don't use shortcuts or tricks that could get your site penalized.

**Q: Do I need a website to do SEO?**
A: Yes. SEO requires a website to optimize. If you don't have one yet, we can build it for you. Our web packages start at competitive prices, and when we build your website, the SEO setup is included at no extra cost.

**Q: What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?**
A: SEO is organic (free clicks, long-term results, takes time to build). Google Ads is paid (instant traffic, costs per click, stops when you stop paying). We recommend both for maximum visibility — SEO for sustainable long-term growth, and Google Ads for immediate leads while SEO builds momentum.

**Q: Will I own the work you do?**
A: Absolutely. Everything we create — content, optimizations, backlinks, accounts — is yours. If you ever decide to stop working with us, you keep everything. We don't hold your website or data hostage.

**Q: How do I know if SEO is working?**
A: We provide monthly reports showing: keyword rankings, organic traffic, backlink growth, Google Business Profile insights, and (most importantly) leads generated. You'll see exactly where your money is going and what results it's producing.

**Q: Can you work with my existing website?**
A: Yes. We can optimize any website built on WordPress, Wix, Shopify, Squarespace, custom HTML, or any other platform. We don't require you to rebuild your site unless it's technically broken.

**Q: What if my business is in a different city?**
A: We serve businesses across Kenya — Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and everywhere in between. SEO is location-agnostic in terms of execution, but location-specific in strategy. We optimize for your specific city and surrounding areas.

**Q: Do you do SEO for international businesses?**
A: Yes. While we specialize in the Kenyan market, we can also optimize for international audiences. If you serve customers outside Kenya, we can target those markets too.

**Q: How often will we communicate?**
A: We provide monthly reports via email and WhatsApp. For Growth and Domination packages, we also have quarterly strategy review calls. You're always welcome to message us on WhatsApp anytime with questions or concerns.
