M-Pesa Integration — The Critical Factor for Kenya

Without M-Pesa, you're losing 70% of potential customers. We build custom M-Pesa integrations for Shopify that work seamlessly:

  • Customer adds products to cart and proceeds to checkout
  • "Pay with M-Pesa" appears as a payment option
  • Customer enters their M-Pesa number
  • STK push sent to their phone (they just enter PIN)
  • Payment confirmed instantly — order marked as paid
  • Both customer and merchant receive confirmation

Our Shopify M-Pesa integration includes: STK push, manual entry fallback, automatic order status updates, refund capability, and transaction reporting. One-time setup cost: KSH 15,000–25,000.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I accept M-Pesa on Shopify?

Yes! Shopify doesn't have native M-Pesa, but we build custom integrations. Your customers see "Pay with M-Pesa" at checkout and receive an STK push on their phone.

How much does a Shopify store cost in Kenya?

Our Shopify development starts at KSH 25,000 (one-time setup). You'll also pay Shopify's monthly fee ($29/month for Basic). M-Pesa integration is a one-time KSH 15,000–25,000.

How long does it take to build a Shopify store?

A basic store takes 1–2 weeks. A custom store with M-Pesa takes 2–4 weeks. The timeline depends on product count and customization level.

Can I manage the store myself?

Yes. Shopify is designed for non-technical users. We provide training on adding products, managing orders, and updating content. Most clients are comfortable within a week.

Do I need a business registration to sell online?

Technically yes for tax compliance, but many small businesses start informally. We recommend getting registered as you grow. We can guide you through the process.
