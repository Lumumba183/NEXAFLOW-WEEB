## 📊 What's Included in Every Package

### 1. Keyword Research
We don't guess. We use data to find the exact words your customers are typing into Google. We analyze:
- Search volume (how many people search monthly)
- Competition (how hard it is to rank)
- Intent (are they ready to buy or just browsing?)
- Local modifiers ("near me," "in Nairobi," "Kenya")

### 2. On-Page SEO
We optimize every element of your pages so Google understands what you offer:
- **Title tags** — compelling, keyword-rich, click-worthy
- **Meta descriptions** — persuasive summaries that drive clicks
- **Header tags** (H1, H2, H3) — logical structure with keywords
- **Image optimization** — compressed, descriptive, alt-tagged
- **Internal linking** — connect related pages to boost authority
- **Schema markup** — structured data for rich snippets in Google

### 3. Technical SEO
The invisible work that makes Google love your site:
- **Site speed** — under 3 seconds load time (critical for mobile users)
- **Mobile optimization** — 80% of Kenyans browse on mobile
- **Crawlability** — fix broken links, redirects, and errors
- **Indexing** — ensure every important page is in Google
- **Security** — SSL certificate, HTTPS, safe browsing
- **Core Web Vitals** — Google's page experience metrics

### 4. Local SEO
Get found when customers search "near me" or "in Nairobi":
- **Google Business Profile** — complete optimization + posts
- **Directory submissions** — Yellow Pages Kenya, Business Listings KE, etc.
- **NAP consistency** — same Name, Address, Phone everywhere
- **Review management** — get more 5-star reviews + respond professionally
- **Local content** — pages targeting "[service] in [location]"

### 5. Content Strategy
Content is how you answer customer questions and rank for long-tail keywords:
- **Blog posts** — keyword-targeted, engaging, shareable
- **Service pages** — detailed, persuasive, conversion-focused
- **FAQ sections** — answer common questions (great for voice search)
- **Comparison pages** — "vs" pages that capture high-intent traffic

### 6. Backlink Building
Links from other websites are votes of confidence. We build quality links from:
- Kenyan business directories
- Industry blogs and publications
- Guest posting opportunities
- Local partnerships and sponsorships
- Social media profiles

### 7. Reporting & Transparency
You'll always know exactly what we're doing and what results you're getting:
- **Monthly rankings report** — where you rank for every target keyword
- **Traffic analysis** — how many visitors, from where, what they do
- **Work log** — exactly what we did this month
- **Next month plan** — what we're doing next and why
